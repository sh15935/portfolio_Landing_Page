About

Our project was inspired by a shared passion for retro-style 2D games and a desire to create an immersive experience for players. We wanted to capture the nostalgia of classic games while incorporating modern elements and innovative gameplay mechanics. The idea originated during a brainstorming session, where we discussed our favorite games and identified the aspects we admired most. This brainstorming process fueled our creative vision and served as the foundation for our project.

Saliha Lammari : Programmer develops the game's code and implements the functionality and translates the game design into programming logic, handles player input, physics, game mechanics, and interactions.

Safae Sahih : Artist creates the visual elements of the game, including character sprites, backgrounds, and animations, designs the user interface (UI) elements, such as menus and buttons,chooses an art style and ensures visual consistency throughout the game.

Dounia Bendib : Game designer creates the game's rules, levels, and objectives.
